Dandelion - Dongdaemun-gu Integrated Civil Complaint Service

Participated in the 2025 K-HTML Hackathon and developed “Dandelion,” a unified civil complaint service for Dongdaemun-gu to address transportation and community issues.

  • Designed and built a dedicated platform to collect, analyze, and visualize resident feedback for actionable policy planning.
  • Developed a full-stack pipeline (frontend, backend, and data visualization) enabling automated workflows from complaint submission to policy reporting.
  • Implemented map-based visualization for traffic complaints and planned phased expansion to lifestyle, safety, and convenience areas.
  • Contributed to creating a data-driven, participatory platform bridging residents and local administration, empowering better governance.
